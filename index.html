Meet The Authors

Pritam Sarkar & Saswati Pal Choudhuri

Dedicated to empowering individuals through knowledge and safer healthcare practices. This guide transforms the "silent risks" of medication into informed awareness, providing a pharmacist-validated resource for daily use.
